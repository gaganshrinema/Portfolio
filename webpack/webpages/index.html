About

1958

Ofer is a known Astrologer, Hermetic Tarot master & a spiritual guide. He was born in 1958 in the city of Tel aviv-jaffa. In his early age he was deeply influenced by the secret books of Kabbalah and the mystery of Coffey reading which was a popular prediction method in the middle east.

1974

Ofer was born into a family known into the occult & mysticism for decades. In his early years he was deep into occult and healing herbs study. He started hi career in predicting people health conditions with reading face photos. He was a nature lover who spend much of his time in traveling between the northern mountains of Israel to the southern desert.

1980

After his marriage in 1980 he studies western astrology, Date of birth numbers while researching the secret old book of Kabbalah "Raziel The Angel". During that period he started to use th Hermetic tarot cards which are base on Kabbalah wisdom and hermetic secret. He then started to become known for his ability to accurately predict people life.

1975

His long research and experience helped him to develop a new approach to reading people natal charts. He is known to read birth chart in a different way than western astrologers. He also use his Hermetic cards to give you a full information of your future life path. Those who consult him for years will testify that he is not only giving accurate life forecast, He will also give you the right solutions to your problems.

1998

1990 to 1999 Ofer had a few media appearances. He was invited to a live radio night podcast and came as a guest at TV live shows. Just when success started to come his way, he felt that something in his life is missing. In late 1999 his life dramatically change. He traveled to Asia where his spiritual journey had began. He spent years in the north India/Nepal Himalaya mountains region, there he was practicing yoga, teaches professional tarot reading and astrology to people from around the world. In the village near where he was staying. You could see tourists and locals coming to his mountain house everyday seeking his tarot and spiritual quidance.

2002

Some of his most famous predictions were publish in Asia early 2001 to 2007, "the rise of the east country's", "the banking crises in USA 2008" and natural disasters in Asia! Some of his past predictions are coming now into reality. Ofer is known for his accurate relationship match up prediction, he helps many young singles to make the right marriage decisions. His honest and straight forward approach become his trade mark.

2004 /5

During the period in north India he was often invited to the bussiness and films city of Mumbai. After constantly traveling back and forth between north and south India he settled in Mumbai. Shortly after he was offered to become the spiritual counselor & reader at the Grand Hayyat Celina which is the largest Hotel in Asia. During this period Ofer became known between the city Celebs and the bussiness community. It was the time he started to make his national media appearances, his success attracted people from all over Asia. His workshop and spiritual events at the Hayyat Spa attracted people who seek his forecast and quidance. It was the time when his name started to be recognize by far community's in USA, Canada and middle east.

2005 /6

Around late 2003 while living between the south beaches of India and the northern mountains region he went through a deep spiritual transformation a period he describe as an enlightenment years. He then started to write his first book "the light" the book was partly written in Automatic writing (sub conciseness writing), he was in a unique state of mind when writing this spiritual manuscript. The book contains an enlightening description of the human soul journey. The book took many years to complete and waited years to be translated to English. "THE LIGHT" was completed during the time of his spiritual work in London and finally brought to publish in 2012.

2009

n early 2009 he was invited by the Movenpick hotels to stay few months at the Movenpick resort and spa in the island Maturities. It was one his most exciting and intensive period, living on a small island resort lecturing teaching and reading people from all over the world.

From 2007 until 2018, 11 years, Ofer was the Astrologer & columnist of the "Hello" Magazine in India, His Monthly and yearly predictions helped the Indian magazine to become very popular! Apart from India he was choen by the "HELLO" Thailand and international "HELLO" Spain to write special yearly predictions to the world royals and celebs. With Notable published Horoscopes in south America, Puerto Rico, Turkey, Serbia, Canada, middle east, Malaysia and more.
