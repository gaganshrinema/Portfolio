AQUARIUS

Work and finance:

The last two years were a very demanding period for your career and work relationships. The present of Saturn in your career sector brought many challenges and tough situations to deal with. You were struggling to get promoted and was force to deal with work colleagues who made your life difficult. hidden competition and unfair changes at your work schedule kept you feeling frustrated. Some of you took on a new job yet eventually end up struggling with the new challenges. others suffered quietly waiting for a better opportunity to arise. Until April 2020 you will not be free to do things your way! later after July you will be able to improve your work statues and find the right way to express your leadership. The best you can do this year is to except the recent work changes and wait until the second half of 2020 to find a better career opportunity. Your financial situation can improve this year if you avoid taking unnecessary risk.be more careful with your spending and more efficient with running the family budget. A decision to invest your savings in stocks or business can end up in a loss, especially if you fail to read correctly the current market conditions.

Love and relationships:

This is a very active and fruitful year especially for those looking for love. You will meet people you never met and if single the chances are you will find a new partner. Marriage life will be stable, your spouse will express his desire to have a child ,move a house or complain about your family issues , do remember to be more caring and avoid being selfish .Venus in retro in May June 2020 and Mars retro in the second half of the year will test new relationship and those who are newly Married !

HOROSCOPE FOR THE YEAR 2020

Aquarius

Work and finance:

2020 will be a interesting yet a complicate year.Saturn enters your sign this year. you will have to take more career responsibility’s and be more efficient in your money management. Saturn is the teacher! It brings restriction and limitation to your life, you will have to learn self-control and need to be more realistic. Those who recently found a new job will feel this pressure. With patient and with using your natural creativity you can easily take over any of these challenges. During the first half of the year you will be busy with long term business plans. February will be difficult month for job seekers due to the Mercury retro weeks. If you do find a new job or starting a new business then it will be wise to wait until April before you start. Due to Venus retro May & June are sensitive months for finance these months can bring delays in your work schedule. apart from all these temporarily aspects you have Neptune in your money sector, Neptune is there to stay until 2025! Neptune can bring illusions, confusion and bad judgment! In this period, you must take advice from professionals avoid taking financial risks and listen to your inner voice! Financial mistakes now can have a long-term effect on you and will be difficult to correct.

Love and relationships:

A very important year for your family life. Singles can suddenly fall in love this year ; some of you will consider marriage. The first half of the year is not recommended time for marriage. The period from July onward is a better time. Relationship that will start after July will quickly tern serious. Uranus in your home and living sector show a possibility of moving a house.do not sell a property before you find your new home! June July will be a difficult month for relationships. During these unstable two months weak relationships can easily break.
